Who we are.

We are a team of innovators within Hexagon, united under the innovation program to simplify internal work processes. Our mission is to develop intelligent, employee-first solutions that enhance everyday productivity while aligning with our organizational goals of compliance, transparency, and operational excellence.

What we do.

We are building a smart chatbot designed to answer your everyday Hexagon-related questions—instantly. Whether you’re searching for training links, export compliance, leave balances, vacation expiries, or even GitHub Copilot guidelines, our chatbot is your go-to solution—eliminating time spent digging through emails, SharePoint sites, or PDF manuals.

Why it matters.

Time is valuable, and the ability to focus on your core work should not be hindered by time-consuming searches for policy documents or process guidelines. By reducing friction in accessing key information, we empower Hexagon employees to make informed decisions faster, ensure audit and compliance readiness (ISO, NQA, etc.), and support a culture of innovation and agility.
